# PriceAgent — AI-Powered Dynamic Pricing for E-commerce

## Problem Statement
E-commerce sellers on marketplaces (Amazon, Bol.com, Zalando) and D2C need to continuously optimize pricing against competitors, demand signals, inventory levels, and margin targets. Most mid-size sellers (EUR 1-20M revenue) use manual pricing or simple rule-based repricing tools that can't adapt to complex market dynamics. Enterprise solutions (Revionics, PROS) cost EUR 50K+/year. The gap: intelligent pricing that considers competitor behavior, demand elasticity, and inventory constraints — at a price point accessible to mid-market sellers.

## Target Customer (ICP)
European e-commerce sellers with EUR 1-20M annual revenue, 500-10K SKUs, selling on 2+ channels. Primary beachhead: Dutch marketplace sellers on Bol.com and Amazon.nl/de. These sellers have enough SKUs that manual pricing is painful but not enough revenue to justify enterprise tools.

## Proposed Solution & Wedge
AI pricing engine that ingests competitor prices, demand data, inventory levels, and margin targets, then recommends or auto-sets optimal prices per SKU per channel. Wedge: Bol.com price optimization — integrate with Bol.com Partner API, monitor competitor pricing on the buy box, and auto-adjust prices to maximize margin while maintaining visibility.

## Pricing Model
- Starter (EUR 149/mo): Up to 500 SKUs, 1 marketplace, daily repricing
- Professional (EUR 499/mo): 5K SKUs, multi-marketplace, real-time repricing, demand forecasting
- Enterprise (EUR 1,500/mo): Unlimited SKUs, custom models, API access, A/B price testing

## Go-to-Market
Bol.com seller communities. Amazon seller forums (NL/DE). E-commerce meetups Amsterdam. Shopify/WooCommerce app store. Content marketing on pricing optimization.

## Defensibility & Moat
Pricing model improves with more transaction data (data flywheel). Multi-marketplace integration creates switching costs. Karel's ML expertise enables sophisticated pricing models. Bol.com-specific optimization is underserved (most repricing tools focus on Amazon US/UK).
