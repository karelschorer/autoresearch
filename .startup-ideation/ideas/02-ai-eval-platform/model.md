# EvalForge — LLM Evaluation & Testing Platform for Enterprise

## Problem Statement
Every company deploying LLMs in production needs to evaluate model quality, detect regressions, and compare providers. But building eval pipelines is a pain: custom harnesses, fragile test suites, no standardized metrics, and results scattered across notebooks. Enterprise teams (banks, insurers, airlines) adopting AI are especially stuck — they need audit trails, reproducibility, and compliance evidence for model decisions. Current eval tools are either open-source frameworks (hard to operationalize) or baked into specific model providers (vendor lock-in).

## Target Customer (ICP)
Mid-to-large enterprises deploying LLMs in production (financial services, healthcare, logistics, legal). Teams of 3-20 ML/AI engineers. Primary beachhead: European enterprises with AI governance requirements (EU AI Act compliance). 

## Proposed Solution & Wedge
Managed LLM evaluation platform: define eval suites, run them across any model provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source), track results over time, generate compliance reports for AI Act. Wedge: pre-built eval templates for regulated industries (financial advice, medical triage, legal analysis) with built-in bias and hallucination detection.

## Pricing Model
- Starter (EUR 199/mo): 10K eval runs/month, 3 model providers, basic dashboard
- Team (EUR 799/mo): 100K eval runs, unlimited providers, CI/CD integration, team collaboration
- Enterprise (EUR 2-5K/mo): Unlimited, custom evals, SOC 2 audit trail, dedicated support

## Go-to-Market
Developer community (open-source eval framework as top-of-funnel). Karel's AF-KLM enterprise network. AI/ML meetups and conferences (Amsterdam ML community). Content marketing on LLM evaluation best practices.

## Defensibility & Moat
Karel has built eval harnesses — knows the pain firsthand. Eval dataset library grows with each customer (network effect). Enterprise compliance reports create switching costs. EU AI Act creates regulatory demand specifically for this type of tooling.
