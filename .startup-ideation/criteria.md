# Validation Criteria — Startup Ideation

## A. Problem Validation — Is the pain real and urgent?

### A1: The problem is a documented time/money sink
**Question:** Is there documented evidence that the target persona spends significant time or money dealing with this problem, OR that failure to solve it causes material harm?
**Pass:** Multiple sources confirm the problem is significant, recurring, and has quantifiable costs (time, money, risk).
**Fail:** Problem is minor, infrequent, or consequences are trivial.

### A2: The problem is growing
**Question:** Is there concrete evidence that the problem is getting worse due to regulatory, market, or technology shifts over the next 2-3 years?
**Pass:** Identifiable trends making the problem more acute (new regulations, market growth, increasing complexity).
**Fail:** Landscape is stable or improving without intervention.

---

## B. Existing Alternatives — Is the current solution bad enough?

### B1: Existing alternatives are inadequate for the target segment
**Question:** Are the existing solutions clearly inadequate — too expensive, too complex, too slow, or too manual for the target customer?
**Pass:** Evidence that current tools/services are priced for enterprises, require heavy integration, or force manual workarounds.
**Fail:** Affordable, effective tools already exist for this segment.

### B2: There is a clear gap between what exists and what the target needs
**Question:** Is there a specific workflow pain that no existing tool addresses well?
**Pass:** Identifiable functional gap — a core task the target persona does that no tool automates or serves.
**Fail:** Existing tools already solve the core workflow at accessible price points.

---

## C. Solution-Market Fit — Can this specific solution win?

### C1: The proposed technology can achieve required quality
**Question:** Can the proposed approach achieve the accuracy, reliability, or performance needed for this use case?
**Pass:** Demonstrated or credibly achievable performance (PoC data, comparable systems, technical analysis showing feasibility).
**Fail:** Technical approach is unproven, unreliable, or insufficient for the domain's requirements.

### C2: There is a narrow wedge that delivers standalone value
**Question:** Can an initial use case deliver clear value without requiring full platform adoption or deep integration?
**Pass:** Self-contained, high-frequency task with clear standalone ROI that can be sold and used independently.
**Fail:** Solution requires deep integration, broad adoption, or multiple modules to deliver value.

---

## D. Market & Economics — Is the market real and reachable?

### D1: There are >=100 identifiable target companies reachable through <=2 channels
**Question:** Can you identify at least 100 target companies/users that fit the ICP and are reachable through identifiable channels?
**Pass:** Communities, associations, events, directories, or platforms with >100 matching targets.
**Fail:** Segment is too small (<50 identifiable targets) or too fragmented to reach efficiently.

### D2: Unit economics work — value delivered >= 3x proposed price
**Question:** Does the value delivered (time saved, risk avoided, revenue enabled) justify at least 3x the proposed price?
**Pass:** Quantifiable value exceeds 3x subscription cost; clear willingness-to-pay signal.
**Fail:** Value is abstract, hard to quantify, or insufficient to justify the price.

---

## E. Founder & Timing — Can this team win now?

### E1: The founding team has a defensible advantage
**Question:** Does Karel + Jeroen's specific combination of skills, experience, and network create a defensible advantage for THIS idea?
**Pass:** Their AI/ML + fintech/exit experience + builder capacity creates a 6+ month head start or unique capability.
**Fail:** No proprietary advantage; any funded competitor replicates in months.

### E2: There is a timing catalyst making NOW the right moment
**Question:** Is there a specific, time-bound catalyst (regulatory change, technology shift, market event) creating urgency in the next 12-24 months?
**Pass:** Identifiable catalyst creating a window of opportunity or spike in demand.
**Fail:** No urgency; opportunity has existed for years or is >3 years away.
