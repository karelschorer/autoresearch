# D2: Unit economics work — value >=3x price

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- Regulatory affairs manager salary in Netherlands: €65,000-€96,000/year. All-in cost: ~€50-75/hour.
- At 15-20 hours/month saved, value = €750-€1,500/month — 2.5-5x the €300-500 price.
- External consultants charge €600-800/day (€75-100/hour). Even 10 hours/month saved = €750-1,000 in avoided costs.
- NVWA fines: €525-€1,050 per standard violation; up to €150,000 for medicinal product misclassification.
- In 2022, a single online drugstore was fined €73,000+ for non-compliant claims.
- In 2025, 25 of 30 investigated collagen sellers had illegal claims (>83% violation rate).
- Packaging non-compliance triggers mandatory redesign: €5,000-50,000+ per event depending on SKU volume.
- France: administrative fines up to €15,000 per product + €1,500 per non-conforming unit.
- Average EU food recall cost: €8-10M (though supplement recalls are smaller scale).
