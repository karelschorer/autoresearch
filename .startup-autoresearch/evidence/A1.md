# A1: Regulatory claim verification is a documented time sink

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- Job postings at EU supplement companies (Vanatari/Berlin, Lonza, Science in Sport) list claims review and marketing copy approval as named primary responsibilities, not incidental tasks.
- The EU health claims list (4,637 original claims; ~2,078 botanical claims "on hold" since 2010) creates a fragmented, multi-source verification requirement that must be cross-referenced per-SKU, per-market.
- Regulatory consultancies describe portfolio-wide claim pre-scans as a standard first engagement step for multi-SKU supplement companies, implying no automated solution exists.
- 40% of compliance teams still use word processors/spreadsheets (NorthRow 2024); software tools promise 90% reduction in manual revision time, confirming the current manual burden is substantial.
- Industry experts (Food Supplements Europe, FoodChain ID) explicitly state that 2025 added significantly to regulatory workloads.
