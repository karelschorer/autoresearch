# C2: Credible narrow wedge for supplements

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- EU supplement products carry an average of 6-7 health claims per product (up to 12 for amino sugar supplements), making claim pre-screening a high-frequency, repetitive task.
- Overall compliance rate is only 48.8-58.7% across EU supplement products; only one-third use exact required wording from Regulation (EU) 432/2012.
- Penalties are material and enforcement is fast: up to €15,000 in administrative fines and per-product market withdrawal.
- Comparable standalone tools (GoVisually, SGS Digicomply) confirm that label claim compliance checking is a commercially validated, separable service.
- The supplement market is large ($87.33B Europe, 2024) and companies already pay per-product, per-country fees to regulatory consultants (e.g., €160.20 per notification in Italy).
- The claim pre-screening task is structurally discrete from broader regulatory workflow — it occurs before production/notification as a front-loaded step.
