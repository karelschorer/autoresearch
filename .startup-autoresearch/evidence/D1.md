# D1: >=100 identifiable target companies reachable through <=2 channels

**Score: 7/10** (dropped from 10 after Cycle 12 stress test)

## Evidence — market exists but conversion is harder than assumed

### The companies exist (unchanged)
- NPN: 100-104 Dutch member companies
- EHPM: 1,600+ EU health-product manufacturers (90% SMEs)
- Vitafoods 2025: 1,455 exhibitors, ~40% finished-product brands (~580 in ICP)
- EU supplement market "moderately fragmented" with many manufacturers

### Stress test: reachable ≠ convertible

**Cold outreach conversion is brutal:**
- Cold email reply rates: 3-5% (down from 8.5% in 2019)
- Meeting-booked rates: 1-2%
- From 1,455 Vitafoods exhibitors, expect 15-30 meetings booked — not 1,455 leads
- Another 20-30% drop-off from meeting to paying customer

**NPN list is gated:**
- NPN member directory is NOT accessible to non-members
- Membership doesn't guarantee contact-level data
- Requires joining the association or scraping LinkedIn/trade show attendee lists

**Discovery channel mismatch:**
- Dominant discovery channels: consultant referrals, peer word-of-mouth, regulatory-trigger events
- Cold outreach is low on the list
- ClaimPilot needs to be embedded in consultant/association ecosystems — takes 12-24 months

**CAC is high early:**
- B2B compliance SaaS targeting SMEs: realistic CAC $500-1,200+
- First-year startup without brand recognition: CAC often $2,000-3,000
- At €299-799/month pricing, payback periods become uncomfortably long

**Sales cycle is long:**
- SME compliance software: 3-6 month sales cycle
- Healthcare/pharma-adjacent buyers: 125-153 days average
- Multi-stakeholder sign-off: marketing + legal/regulatory + finance
- First 10 customers: plan for no revenue until Month 9-12

### Why 7/10
The target companies exist in large numbers — that part is solid. But the criterion asks about "reachable through <=2 channels." The stress test reveals that reaching them (knowing they exist) is not the same as converting them (getting them to pay). The gated NPN list, high CAC, long sales cycles, and consultant-dominated discovery channels mean the path to first 10 customers is harder and slower than the 10/10 score implied.
