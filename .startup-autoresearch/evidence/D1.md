# D1: >=100 identifiable target companies reachable through <=2 channels

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- NPN (Netherlands) has approximately 100-104 member companies — meeting the threshold from a single national association alone.
- EHPM represents over 1,600 health-product manufacturers across 17 EU countries through 14 national associations. Over 90% are SMEs matching the ICP.
- Food Supplements Europe has 14 national member associations and ~16 direct corporate members.
- Vitafoods Europe 2025 had 1,455 exhibiting companies from 69 countries; approximately 40% are finished-product brands (~580 companies directly in ICP range).
- 60% of supplement manufacturers report delays of 6-18 months in product launches due to varying national compliance requirements.
- The EU supplement market is described as "moderately fragmented" with a "large number of manufacturers."
