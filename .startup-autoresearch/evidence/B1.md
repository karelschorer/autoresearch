# B1: Existing alternatives are inadequate for mid-market

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- SAP EHS is a Tier 1 ERP solution with no public pricing; requires custom quotes through authorized partners. Clearly scoped for large enterprises with high complexity.
- Veeva Vault RIM targets life sciences (pharma/biotech) and is enterprise-priced; not designed for mid-size FMCG claim verification.
- Food compliance tools on Capterra/G2 (FoodDocs, SafetyChain, GoAudits, NORMEX) focus on HACCP, FSMA, food safety inspections, and GMP — NOT on marketing/health claim verification against EU Register.
- External food regulatory consultants in NL/EU charge €600-800/day (€75-100/hour). A company needing 15-20 hours/month of claim verification pays €1,125-2,000/month for consultancy.
- No affordable, mid-market tool was identified that specifically addresses EU nutrition/health claim pre-screening with regulatory traceability.
- SGS DigiComply covers broad food safety/regulatory intelligence but is a comprehensive platform, not a focused claim verification tool.
