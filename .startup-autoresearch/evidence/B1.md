# B1: Existing alternatives are inadequate for mid-market

**Score: 9/10** (improved from 8 in Cycle 9 — direct workflow evidence)

## Evidence (updated Cycle 9)

### The manual reality is confirmed by industry insiders
- **Rubicon Bridge** (compliance tech company) explicitly describes mid-size supplement companies using "comprehensive spreadsheets cataloging permitted substances, allowable usage levels, and mandatory warnings" as the **standard approach**. Notes it is "labor-intensive, susceptible to errors."
- The EU Commission provides the Register as a **downloadable Excel file** — confirming this is the workflow regulators assume companies use.

### Named tools that exist — and why they miss
| Tool | What it does | Why it misses |
|---|---|---|
| Veeva Vault RIM | Pharma registration tracking | Min ~$50K/year; not for food claims |
| SAP EHS | Enterprise ERP compliance | Tier 1 ERP, not for mid-market |
| FoodChainID/Nutraveris | Full label audit + multi-country | Enterprise-tier, no self-serve |
| Ecomundo | EU supplement registration + consulting | Consulting model (by-the-hour) |
| CIKLab | QC/batch tracking for nutraceuticals | €47/mo but solves QC, not claims |
| SGS DigiComply | Regulatory intelligence 150+ countries | Broad monitoring, not claim pre-screening |
| Trace One | Regulatory database 170 countries | Formulation compliance, not claim text |

### No affordable claim-text verification tool exists
- No tool was found where a mid-market regulatory manager can paste a marketing claim and get an instant EU Register match with conditions of use and traceability.
- Multiple targeted searches across G2, Capterra, Gartner, and industry forums confirmed this gap.
- The only sub-€500/mo tool (CIKLab) solves a completely different problem (batch QC).

### Consultancy rates are prohibitive for ongoing use
- Senior EU regulatory consultants: $225-300/hour on freelance platforms (Kolabtree)
- Mid-level food regulatory consultants: €75-150/hour
- At 15-20 hours/month of claim verification, consultancy costs €1,125-3,000/month

### Why 9/10
The evidence is now very strong: Rubicon Bridge's insider account confirms spreadsheets are the norm, no affordable claim-text tool exists, and consultancy rates are prohibitive. The 9/10 ceiling is because some adjacent tools (FoodChainID, Trustwell/AskReg) could add claim pre-screening as a feature — the gap is real but not guaranteed to remain unfilled.
