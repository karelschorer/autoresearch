# B2: There is a gap between current tools and what mid-market needs

**Verdict: YES**

## Evidence

- Existing RIMS platforms (Veeva Vault, MasterControl) focus on document management, submission tracking, and registration lifecycle — not on real-time claim pre-screening.
- Food safety compliance tools (FoodDocs, SafetyChain, GoAudits) focus on HACCP plans, GMP audits, food safety inspections — not on marketing claim verification against regulatory databases.
- The EU Register of authorized health claims is a static database; no existing tool was found that cross-references a company's specific marketing claims against the Register + EFSA opinions + conditions of use in an automated pipeline.
- SGS DigiComply offers regulatory intelligence and monitoring but focuses on regulatory change tracking, not claim-level verdict generation with traceability.
- GoVisually claims 99.9% accuracy on label keyword verification but is focused on label artwork proofing, not regulatory claim substantiation.
- The gap is specifically: taking a marketing claim → checking it against the permitted claims list → verifying conditions of use → producing a verdict with regulatory citations. No mid-market tool does this.
