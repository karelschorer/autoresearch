# B2: There is a gap between current tools and what mid-market needs

**Score: 9/10** (strengthened in Cycle 19 — deep competitive analysis confirms gap)

## Evidence (updated Cycle 19)

### The gap is confirmed by exhaustive competitive mapping
Every existing tool works in the WRONG DIRECTION or covers the WRONG market:
- **FoodChainID:** Formulation-in, claims-out. Does not analyze marketing copy.
- **Apex Compliance:** Marketing copy scanning, but US-only (FDA/FTC) and keyword-based (not semantic). No EU health claims coverage.
- **Trustwell/AskReg:** Q&A chatbot. Cannot ingest documents for bulk screening.
- **SGS DigiComply (Labelwise):** Label artwork validation. Enterprise-only. Health claims secondary.
- **Rubicon Bridge:** Formulation-first compliance. Does not analyze claim text.
- **RIMS platforms (Veeva, MasterControl):** Document management, not claim pre-screening.
- **Food safety tools (FoodDocs, SafetyChain):** HACCP/GMP, not marketing claims.

### The gap is specifically:
Taking **arbitrary marketing copy** → using **NLP/AI** to identify health claims (including paraphrased/creative wording) → cross-referencing against **EU Register + conditions of use** → producing a **verdict with regulatory citations** + **suggesting compliant alternatives**

### The FoodChainID + Apex partnership validates the gap
The market leader in formulation compliance (FoodChainID) had to partner with a separate tool (Apex) for marketing content review — and Apex only covers US regulations with keyword matching. This proves: (1) the formulation tools know they can't do claim-text analysis, (2) the only claim-text tool is US-only and keyword-based.

### Why 9/10
The competitive gap is the strongest it has ever been — confirmed through deep analysis of 7 competitors. No tool on the market does AI-powered EU claim-text pre-screening with semantic analysis. The 9/10 ceiling: Apex could expand to EU, FoodChainID could add NLP, or a new entrant could emerge. The gap is real today but not permanently defensible without execution speed.
