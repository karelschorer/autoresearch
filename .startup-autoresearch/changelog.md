**Score:** 9/10
**Mutation operator:** reposition_value
**Change:** Repositioned solution from "AI delivers verdict" to "AI pre-screens + human expert validates" — explicitly framing ClaimPilot as AI-assisted, not AI-decided
**Hypothesis:** The C1 failure was partly a framing problem. The business model already included "expert shadow review" but didn't emphasize it.
