# C2: Wedge delivers standalone value
**Score: 5/10** (updated Cycle 3, DOWN from 6)

## Evidence

### Marketing compliance wedge (ABANDONED)
- Sedric AI already does this for 500+ financial institutions
- RegTech100 2026, HSBC-backed, 40+ languages
- This wedge is occupied — cannot compete

### White paper generation wedge (CURRENT — PROBLEMATIC)
- MiCA white papers are mandatory for token offerings
- Complex requirements (Article 6, iXBRL format, sustainability disclosures)
- No fully automated tool exists — gap is real
- Law firm cost: estimated EUR 5-15K per white paper

### Critical problem: frequency
- White papers are **one-time deliverables**, not recurring tasks
- Updates only required for "material changes"
- A CASP offering one token writes one white paper — then the work is done
- Not a monthly SaaS workflow. Hard to build recurring revenue.
- This is more of a consulting/project engagement than a SaaS product

### Potential expanded wedge
- **Ongoing MiCA compliance monitoring** — regulatory change tracking + impact assessment specific to CASP license type
- **Periodic reporting automation** — CASPs must file regular reports to NCAs (transaction data, complaints, incidents)
- **Multi-token management** — exchanges listing many tokens need white papers for each
- The recurring value may be in the compliance *maintenance* not the compliance *setup*

### Why dropped to 5
The disclosure generation wedge is a real gap but a poor SaaS fit — it's a one-time project per token, not a recurring workflow. Need to find a wedge that's both (a) an unserved gap AND (b) a recurring, high-frequency task justifying monthly subscription.
