# A1: MiCA compliance is a documented burden
**Score: 8/10** (Cycle 0 baseline)

## Evidence
MiCA compliance is mandatory and new. Every EU crypto firm must comply or lose its license. Compliance teams are small and overwhelmed by novel regulation. DNB/BaFin are actively processing license applications.

## Why not higher
The crypto industry is small and some firms are outsourcing to law firms rather than building internal compliance. Need to quantify the actual compliance cost per firm.
