# D2: Unit economics work
**Score: 8/10** (updated Cycle 5, UP from 7)

## Evidence

### Compliance cost data (hard numbers)
- **35% of blockchain startups** estimate compliance costs will exceed **$500,000/year** under MiCA
- Large exchanges: annual MiCA compliance cost exceeds **EUR 500,000** (licensing, reporting, monitoring)
- **73% of crypto firms** investing in AI and blockchain analytics tools for compliance
- **40%+ of exchanges** reported difficulty meeting reporting requirements due to high costs
- **25% of global crypto firms** plan to withdraw from EU due to MiCA costs
- EMT custody may require both MiCA + PSD2 licenses — **doubling** compliance costs

### Penalty context (the stick)
- Regulatory fines projected to surpass **EUR 1.2B in 2025**
- Per-violation penalties: **EUR 50K to EUR 5M**
- France: **EUR 62M fine** against a single platform (largest to date)
- **EUR 540M+** in fines issued since implementation
- **50+ license revocations** through Feb 2025
- Non-compliant exchanges lost **40% of EU user base**

### Value proposition math
For a mid-market CASP spending EUR 200-500K/year on compliance:
- **MiCAGuard at EUR 999/mo (EUR 12K/year)** = 2.4-6% of compliance budget
- If the tool saves even 10% of compliance team time → EUR 20-50K saved → **2-4x ROI**
- If the tool prevents even ONE fine (min EUR 50K) → **4x+ ROI**
- Compared to Big Four advisory retainer (EUR 50-200K/year): massive saving
- 73% of firms already investing in compliance tech — willingness-to-pay signal is strong

### Why 8 and not higher
- The EUR 200-500K compliance cost applies to LARGE exchanges; mid-market CASPs (ICP) may spend EUR 50-150K (1-3 person team × EUR 50-80K salary + external advisory)
- At the lower end (EUR 50K total compliance spend), EUR 12K/year is 24% of budget — harder sell
- Crypto firms are famously cost-sensitive, especially post-bear-market
- Need to validate that mid-market CASPs see EUR 999/mo as justified vs. DIY spreadsheets

### Pricing model implication
The EUR 299/mo Starter may be the right entry point for mid-market CASPs with <EUR 100K compliance budget. The EUR 999/mo Pro tier targets firms spending EUR 200K+. Enterprise (EUR 3-5K/mo) targets the larger exchanges.
