# B1: Existing alternatives are inadequate
**Score: 5/10** (updated Cycle 2, DOWN from 6)

## Evidence

### Competitive landscape — more developed than assumed

The crypto compliance RegTech space has MULTIPLE well-funded players, organized in three layers:

| Layer | Players | MiCA Coverage |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| **Identity/KYC** | Sumsub (unicorn, $1B+ valuation), Muinmos | KYC/KYB onboarding for MiCA CASPs |
| **Transaction monitoring** | Chainalysis ($34B traced), Elliptic, TRM Labs | AML/blockchain analytics |
| **Travel Rule** | Notabene (1,200+ VASP network) | FATF Travel Rule compliance |
| **Marketing compliance** | **Sedric AI** (500+ financial institutions, RegTech100 2026) | MiCA marketing monitoring |
| **Regulatory intelligence** | Apiax, Muinmos | Cross-border rules, onboarding |

### CRITICAL: Sedric AI is a direct competitor for the marketing compliance wedge

Sedric automatically scans websites, ads, emails, social media for MiCA marketing violations. Key facts:
- **Already serves 500+ financial institutions** (not just crypto)
- Recognized in **RegTech100 2026**
- Secured venture loan from **HSBC Innovation Banking**
- Monitors in **40+ languages**
- **Adaptive detection** — updates models based on new ESMA/NCA guidance
- Specifically targets MiCA marketing compliance for crypto firms
- Has LLM-powered analysis that "understands nuance and natural speech"

This is NOT a gap — Sedric is doing exactly what MiCAGuard's marketing compliance wedge proposed. The wedge is already occupied.

### What IS still a gap
- **MiCA-specific disclosure generation** (crypto-asset white papers, risk assessments) — no tool automates this
- **MiCA regulatory change tracking** specific to individual firm's license type and services — Apiax does cross-border rules but not MiCA-specific change monitoring
- **Integrated MiCA compliance dashboard** combining marketing + disclosures + reporting — no single tool does this

### Why score dropped to 5
The original B1 score of 6 was based on the assumption that "MiCA marketing compliance" was an underserved wedge. Sedric AI directly invalidates this. The broader crypto compliance space has established players in every layer. The "gap" is narrower than previously scored — it's in disclosure generation and integrated MiCA-specific tooling, not marketing scanning.
