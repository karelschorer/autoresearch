# B2: Gap exists in MiCA compliance tooling
**Score: 6/10** (unchanged Cycle 3)

## Evidence

### White paper / disclosure generation
- MiCA white papers are mandatory for token offerings (Article 4/5/6)
- Specific content requirements: issuer info, project description, rights/obligations, risks, sustainability disclosures, management liability statement
- Must be in iXBRL machine-readable format (ESMA technical standard)
- Three categories with different requirements: EMTs, ARTs, "Other"
- Non-compliance penalty: up to EUR 700,000

### Existing solutions for white paper generation
- **CCRI (mica-whitepaper.com)** — free templates, but cannot produce compliant white paper on its own
- **MiCA Crypto Alliance** — dedicated drafting service
- **Law firms** (LEXR, CERHA HEMPEL, Inteliumlaw) — end-to-end white paper + NCA notification
- Law firm pricing: EUR 310/hr (associate rate) × 2+ weeks = EUR ~5-15K estimated

### The gap is real but narrowing
- No fully automated AI tool generates MiCA-compliant white papers from structured input
- Templates exist (CCRI) but still require extensive manual work
- Law firms do it manually — ripe for AI augmentation
- The gap: automated draft generation with regulatory traceability to specific MiCA articles and ESMA technical standards

### Critical weakness: one-time deliverable
- White papers are published once per token offering/admission to trading
- Updates required only for "material changes" (not monthly)
- This is a project-based deliverable, not a recurring SaaS workflow
- Hard to build MRR on one-time deliverables unless the firm offers multiple tokens or the scope expands
