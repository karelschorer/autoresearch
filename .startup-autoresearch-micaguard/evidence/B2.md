# B2: Gap exists in MiCA compliance tooling
**Score: 6/10** (Cycle 0 baseline)

## Evidence
The marketing compliance angle is somewhat novel, but MiCA marketing rules are simpler than EU health claims. A generic LLM could handle most marketing review. The gap is narrower than it looks — the hard MiCA compliance problems (reserve management, prudential requirements) are not what this tool addresses.

## Why not higher
Need to determine whether the gap is in marketing compliance (simple rules) or in a more complex area where AI adds genuine value over generic tools.
