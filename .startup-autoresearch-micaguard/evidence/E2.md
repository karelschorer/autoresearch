# E2: Timing catalyst
**Score: 8/10** (Cycle 0 baseline)

## Evidence
MiCA full effectiveness (Jan 2026) is the catalyst. ESMA technical standards still being published. The compliance burden is peaking right now. However, this is a one-time compliance wave — once firms are compliant, ongoing monitoring is lower-effort.

## Why not higher
The timing window is strong but potentially finite. Need to determine if MiCA creates sustained ongoing compliance work or just a one-time implementation wave.
