# E2: Timing catalyst
**Score: 9/10** (updated Cycle 6, UP from 8)

## Evidence

### MiCA is NOT a one-time compliance wave — it's a sustained regulatory expansion

**Level 2/3 measures still being published (2026):**
- ESMA has delivered 30+ Technical Standards and Guidelines, with more coming
- March 2026: New guidelines on security protocols, crypto-asset classification, market abuse detection, financial instrument qualification
- January 2026: Staff knowledge and competence guidelines
- June 2026: Delegated Act on Article 139 (technical definitions), Commission report on delegated acts power
- Mid-2026: Interim register formally integrated into ESMA IT systems

**Additional regulation layers compounding through 2027:**
- **CARF/DAC8** (Jan 2026): Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework — CASPs must collect detailed user data for tax reporting, first cross-border exchange in 2027
- **PSD2 interplay** (March 2026): EMT custody/transfers may require dual licensing (MiCA + PSD2) — compliance cost doubling
- **AMLA** (2026-2027): Anti-Money Laundering Authority rolling out new AML/CFT guidelines and RTS
- **AMLR/AMLD6** (July 2027): New AML Regulation applies — another compliance layer
- **December 2027**: Trading platforms must have white papers for pre-MiCA crypto-assets

**Key deadlines creating ongoing urgency:**
| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| July 1, 2026 | ALL grandfathering ends — final deadline |
| June 30, 2026 | Commission delegated act on definitions |
| June 30, 2027 | Commission report on MiCA application (potential legislative proposal) |
| July 10, 2027 | AMLR applies |
| Dec 31, 2027 | White papers for pre-MiCA tokens |

### Why 9 and not 10
- The timing is genuinely strong and sustained — not a one-time wave
- Multiple converging regulatory timelines create compound urgency through 2027
- The compliance burden is GROWING, not stabilizing
- Not a 10 because: the enforcement intensity from NCAs is still uneven (some aggressive, some passive); the regulatory expansion may slow post-2027 if the framework matures

### Stress test: "What if MiCA stabilizes and demand drops?"
Even if MiCA's core framework stabilizes, CARF/DAC8, AMLA, and AMLR create NEW compliance layers through 2027. Each new regulation requires updates to policies, procedures, reporting, and technology. A compliance operations platform gains value as the regulatory stack deepens — it becomes the single pane of glass for managing all crypto-related regulations, not just MiCA.
